About my podcast

Seeds of Faith buds from the idea of motivating the listeners through inspiring episodes that have God at the centre of the learnings. My experiences have showed me the importance of faith in all spheres of life. You need faith when you apply for a course, when you are ill, when you water a flower. Maybe you are facing a harship in your family or at your work-place, or perhaps you are simply searching for inner peace. Good news: you are in the right place. I created this podcast with the intention of launching an alternative source of encouragement that would encompass all aspects of daily life, dealing with various topics that can be of everyone's interest.

If you are at a great stage in your relationship with God, this podcast can strengthen your faith even more. If you are in a limbo, or you feel a bit lost in your path, then this podcast can lead you to find the Pastor's voice, calling you. If you are have never had an encounter with God, then this podcast can give you an insight in who He is and how much He loves you.
The name: In origin, Seeds of Faith was inspired by Matthew 17:20, which shows the immense value of faith. "Truly I tell you: if you have faith as small as a mustard seed, you can say to this mountain, «Move from here to there», and it will move. Nothing will be impossible for you." Imagine, if our faith in the Lord was as small as a seed, nothing could bring us down. The invincibility that comes from letting God be the Captain of our life is incomparable. It is far more precious than gold and way more fulfilling than any material possession. Faith is considered one of the three most significant values (1 Corinthians 13), only subordinate to love. For this reason, I envisioned my podcast as a mini-manual to consult when spiritual health is weak; as a bag of mustard seeds, where you can search for the one that will make your plant grow vigorous.

About Francesca


I am an ambitious and enthusiastic woman, with a handful of interests and passions. I see the world through colorful lenses, even when I am in the middle of a storm. I can find a clear cloud somewhere in the sky. That clear cloud in my horizon has always been God, who never failed me. God has faught my battles, has defended my cases, has sheltered me, has been my comfort and my rock. Professionally, I am a translator and an interpreter; I also work as a private tutor and as a freelancer in all areas that concern languages. Polyglot by choice, I am fluent in English, Spanish and French, whereas my native language is Italian. I graduated at University of Southampton (UK) and also studied at Université Grenoble Alpes (France) for a year placement. Whether it's an economics journal article or poetry, I love reading and learning. I have my own blog where I paint out my thoughts; you can find it here: https://life-360.webnode.com/. Being a very creative person, I find beauty in pretty much everything. I have a passion for cooking, baking, dancing and drawing. Travelling is always on top of my list of things to do. My cross-national experiences of volunteering and leisure have shaped me into the person I am today. You will find out more about me by listening to my podcast.
